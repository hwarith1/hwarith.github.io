Teaching

At Princeton, I have been privileged to serve as an Associate Instructor for courses in comparative politics, and I am constantly looking for ways to grow as an instructor and scientific communicator. In Spring 2026, I completed the Princeton McGraw Center’s Teaching Transcript Program — a multi-year training program including in-class observation by McGraw staff and workshops in contemporary pedagogy — which helped me turn my experiences in the classroom into a set of generalizable insights that inform my teaching philosophy.
